Our services

Bringing studio quality to the stage.

Polsonics offers a wide array of highly customized audio solutions for live shows, concerts and corporate events. Our services include live sound mixing and reinforcement, audio recording, and PA systems. We combine the best quality brand equipment, fine-tuned live sound mixing techniques, and the latest technology, in order to create the best experience for any live audience.

Interested in hiring us for your next event? You can email us at cs@polsonics.com, visit our Facebook page, or fill out the contact form below.

About us

Meet the team behind the magic.

We are a team of passionate, music-lovers who came together to bring the best audio quality to a live audience.

We have seen great artists perform with mediocre audio systems, or mega shows with tons of equipment that produce high volume with poor sound quality. Having seen this, we made it our mission to bring the best audio quality possible to live shows.
